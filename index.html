Providing Extraordinary Grimms Fairy Tales

For the last several years, I have provided ideas to many individuals and non-profit organizations. Some of the ideas came to creating this webpage for children to read fairy tales on the go without having to use many physical books and take up space. This is here to assist in limiting how much space is taken up on the road.

On this website, you'll find four stories from the Brothers Grimm. The four stories are:

  • Hansel & Gretel
  • Rapunzel
  • The Frog-Prince
  • The Traveling Musicians

    • These stories, along with all of the other fairy tales written by the Borthers Grimm, are freely available through Project Guttenberg (http://www.gutenberg.org/), the world's largest collection of free electronic books. The four stories have delighted generations of children and we hope you enjoy them as well. All of the stories are "By: The Brothers Grimm."

    The continent of Westeros is known for its wonderful people and cozy charm. Although, there a number of less-known perks that are worth noting if you're planning a visit.

    This website provides a brief glimpse into some of the most wonderful hidden gems of the wonderful place.
